Where I'm From

Leeds

Leeds is the city where I was Born/ come from. It is the UK's third largest city after London and birmingham. The city is part of Yorkshire in the north of England. The city was formed in 1207 built by the River Aire. The city was small up untill the industrial revolution where the city was made more important and started to rapidly grow. Building a canel that would transport good west to Liverpool and the uses of the river to transport goods east to hull. Outside of London it is the second for finance, having a lot of business move there after the second world war.

Yorkshire

Yorkshire is a historic county in northern England. It’s known for its Roman and Viking heritage, as well as its Norman castles, medieval abbeys, Industrial Revolution-era cities and 2 national parks. The county was found over 1000 years ago. Represented by the white rose that comes from the war of the roses in the 15th century. A war that was against Lancashire who are represented by the red rose a rivalry that still exist today. Yorkshire is home to cities such as Leeds, Sheffield, Hull and York the city New York is Name after. York is one of the most historical cities in the UK being nearly 2000 years old and once being the capital of the country.

Leeds United

Leeds United is the Football club (soccer) I support. The club was formed in 1919 following the disbanding of two teams in the city. Sadly, the teams have been through some hard times, once being considered the best English football team and could even rival most international teams. The team currently play at the Elland Road Stadium with the manager of Marcelo Biles. The team colours are white, blue and yellow based on the Yorkshire flags colours, even including the Yorkshire rose on the team’s logo.

Northumbria University

Back in England, I study at Northumbria University. The University is in Newcastle upon Tyne in the North East of England. I have been studying here since September 2015, studying the subject Information Technology and Management for business. During my studies, I have developed knowledge and skills in the following key areas: Database design, Spreadsheets, Web Development, Big Data and Java. Compared to TCNJ my university looks very different, being in a city centre, the builder is a lot closer together and even some department is at another campus 20 minutes away.
