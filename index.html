Welcome

I’m Daniel McGlinchey, I’ am currently an Exchange student from England. I was born in the city of Leeds in the county of Yorkshire, this is in the north of England. Back in England, I study at Northumbria University, located in Newcastle. Where I study Information Technology and Management for business. I started at Northumbria in September of 2015 and I plan to go back there in September to finish my degree. During my studies at Northumbria I have developed knowledge and skills in the following key areas: Database design, Spreadsheets, Web Development, Big Data and Java. I plan to keep developing upon these skills and add more skills to my list.




For this Academic year, I spent the first half working in industry for my chosen field of study and the second half is studying aboard, here in New Jersey. In my first semester, I spent it doing a work placement at the company SPI (Styrene Packaging & Insulation Ltd), which is the UK largest producer of styrene. For this job, I worked in the office providing IT support and working on the website. Some of the duties that were included for IT support; setting email accounts for staff, their computer and wiring devices to the network. This was a nine to four job require me to work thirty-five hours a week.



Currently, I’m studying at The College of New Jersey for a semester, I arrived here on the 23rd of January and will leave in May. The subjects that I have chosen are like what I’m doing back at home, having subjects that include IT and business.
