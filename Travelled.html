Travelled Locations

New York

Visiting New York allowed me to visit famous landmarks in the US. Going on Staten Island ferry, allowing me to view the statue of liberty and view the New York skyline. Going through the rest of the city gave me a chance to see famous locations like the grand central station, little Italy, the New York supreme courthouse and the empire state building. allowing me to walk past famous locations that I have see in movie and TV shows before.

Boston

The last few Days of Spring break I went to Boston. A city that you can it see has a lot of history. I was able to visit famous locations of America an even saw Bunker which I walked up all 300 steps, but close to the top, it was more like crawling to the top. The Day of arriving there I spent it doing the freedom trail, checking out famous locations of the American revolution.

Philadelphia

When my family came over to visit me we headed to Philadelphia for a couple of days. During my time there I was able to visit locations as the Liberty Bell and as the Philadelphia Museum of Art where Rocky famously ran up. It nice seeing Philadelphia seeing how it compares to New York give a better idea of an American city.

Canada

During the start of Spring Break, I went up to Canada to visit my friend from home, who is studying at Brock University. This trip gave me a chance to go visit Toronto and experience what a Canadian city is like. During my time there I also got the chance to see Niagara Falls, a lot of it was still frosted over making it look like something out of this world. giving me the feeling like I was at the end of the world.
