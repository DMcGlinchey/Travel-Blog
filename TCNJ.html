The College New Jersey

Currently, I’m studying at The College of New Jersey for a semester, I arrived here on the 23rd of January and will leave in May. Studying abroad was something I’ve always wanted to do and has been an amazing opportunity. I've known I was coming here since March 2018 when I got my acceptance letter. I choose TCNJ for its area, giving me the chance to visit locations like New York City and Philadelphia. I also choose TNCJ because it stood out to me more than the other university options I had. The subjects that I have chosen are similar to what I’m doing back at home, having subjects that include IT and business.

  • Chosen Subjects
  • Cs I for Science/Engnrg
  • Web Design and Development
  • Management Principles & Practice
  • Organizational Behavior

Being here has allowed me to experience what it like to study in the US and in another country. TCNJ and my university from home have a lot of similarities, but there is a lot of things that are different. How they differ is at home I choose one course similar to a major here. At first when picking my subjects and even starting here I thought it was strange choosing multiple subjects that can be completely different from one another. It was strange at the start that TCNJ isn't in a city, being so used to being in a city at my home and university, it felt like a big change.
